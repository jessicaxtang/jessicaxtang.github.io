about me

I am always excited to explore, try, and learn... whether it's new perspectives, projects, or beverages at coffee shops. Since eleventh grade, I've been especially intrigued by the field of artificial intelligence, especially its application in neuroscience. Explore my hub to find out more about my past projects, teaching experience, and my hobbies!

Currently,

I'm a researcher at the Cognitive Neuroscience and Sensorimotor Integration (CoNSens) Lab at UofT, where we are investigating the robotic classification and grasping predictions of objects, by training convolutional neural networks to model the ventral and dorsal stream as biologically accurate as we can.

As the founder of illuminaite academy, my team and I continue to commit to our mission and goal of demystifying computer science and artificial intelligence concepts to students of all ages, backgrounds, and programs. Starting September 2023, illuminaite is expanding to several universities across Canada including UofT, UBC, McMaster, York, SFU, and Western! We hope to increase our impact and reach as we bring together students from computer science, engineering, to political science programs.
